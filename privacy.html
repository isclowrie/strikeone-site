Privacy Policy – StrikeOne

StrikeOne does not collect, store, or share personal data from users.

The app uses Apple iCloud to securely sync game, player, and team data across your personal devices. This information is stored in your iCloud account and is never transmitted to external servers or accessed by the developer.

No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking is used in StrikeOne.

Any information shared with other users (such as team members or parents) is done at your discretion within the app.
